My name is Felix, and I'm from the Mexican state of Chihuahua, which is close to El Paso, Texas. I consider myself to be a laid-back individual that enjoys conversing with people from all walks of life.

I already have a master's degree in biotechnology and a bachelor's degree in clinical chemistry. These degrees enabled me to work as a forensic scientist in the field of genetics for five years in Mexico. I come into contact with a lot of software during my time at school and at work, and I've felt inclined to learn programming since then.

I currently work in sales, but I hope to have the opportunity to teach in the future. Video gaming, reading, and anime are some of my favorite pastimes.
